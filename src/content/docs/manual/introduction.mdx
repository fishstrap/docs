Welcome to Fishstrap, a custom bootstrapper for Roblox. Fishstrap gives you a quick and easy way to modify your Roblox experience. Some of the features we offer include:

- Discord Rich Presence
- Customizable appearance and theme options
- Custom Integrations
- Modding
- Roblox Studio support
- Flag editor (Roblox Studio only)
- Cache cleaner

...and many more!
